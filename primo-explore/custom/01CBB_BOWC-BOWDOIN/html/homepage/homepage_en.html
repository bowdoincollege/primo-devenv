What Am I Searching?

After entering a search term(s), select the scope of your search in the drop-down list:

Almost everything
Broadest possible search; articles, books, ebooks, sound recordings, film, and more in Colby, Bates, and Bowdoin (CBB) Libraries. Newspapers not included by default.
CBB Libraries
Books, ebooks, sound recordings, film, and more in CBB Libraries.
Articles and more
Articles, chapters of books and ebooks, reviews, and full text in Bowdoin databases. To add newspapers, do a search, then select “Newspaper search” on left side of your results page.
Course Reserves
Course materials by course name, number, instructor, or title.
Special Collections & Archives
Rare books, artists' books, archives, manuscripts, and other materials in Special Collections & Archives.
